A bit about Aiden

Aiden Sinclair

Aiden is an artist he draws, he paints, but prefers illustration. In Harts Michigan where Aiden was born, is also where he started to draw at a very young age. When he was 12 his mother, Raye had to make a tough decision to relocate, to find a career and build Aiden a better life.


Mixing It Up

That decision landed Aiden in a small city located in Iowa. Through the tough times of transitioning Aiden always had his art. As he grew so did his love for art, and so he took an art class in high school. There he broadened his knowledge of the tools that artist use and learned what worked best for his style.


Fun facts about Aiden

Aiden shares a birthday with Walt Disney exactly 100 years apart. Walt Disney was born December 5th, 1901 and Aiden Sinclair was born December 5th, 2001. Aiden loves a well-prepared cheeseburger it's his favorite food, his favorite color is yellow, and one of his favorite artis is Vincent Van Gogh.


Art Supplies

Some of the tools Aiden uses are paint brushes, markers, watercolors, and acrylic paint with super bright colors. Occasionally Aiden shops locally but he orders most of his art supplies online. He said he would mention his favorite art store if they would give him free supplies for life.
