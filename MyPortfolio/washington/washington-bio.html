The Making of Verlee Washington



I am proud to be a Midwest bread, corn fed, Iowa & Illinois native. I come from a huge family, 10 uncles, 9 are my mother’s brothers (one is her twin) and 2 aunts. One was my mothers sister, the other was my fathers sister, and there were too many to count by marriage. I grew up with 7 siblings and over 60 of my cousins.

I raised three beautiful children here, which are all off creating their own lives. And almost 2 years ago, one of them gifted me the greatest gift ever, I call him Granduncan. He was born 2 days before my birthday; it is the best birthday gift ever.

I am an early adopter of technology, since back in the, “You’ve got mail” days. So, it was no surprise to my husband, when our children left the house, that I decided to go back to school and change my career. I started taking IT courses at our local college, graduated cum laude with my associates, landed a great career, and never looked back.

Currently I attend Bellevue University, I am working on attaining my full-stack development degree. I love what I am learning and can barely contain my excitement to be released into the web developer field! Hello World!
